ORHAN LAB INTERACTION

Orhan A lives and works between two studios in New York and Bombay City.
His work spans a variety of media including print making, animation, fashion illustration
and the art of the moving image. He has dirrected print, motion and interactive projects for business,
non-profit and cultural clients worldwide. A five year long documentary that Orhan co-produced, entitled "Life by Orry" won a Nickelodeon
choice award in 2008 and is distributed internationally. Currently Orhan's self-initiated work rages from limited edition perfume
and self signed objects to hand printed textiles and cosmetics. Orhan also teaches class in the
arts and Science and Time-based media for the Kodaikanal International School
and its branches of orphanages, including but not limited to Bethania.
